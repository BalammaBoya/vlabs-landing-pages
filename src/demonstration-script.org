** Scenarios :
   Following are the various scenarios which demonstrates
   the =beta= version of =vlabs-landing-pages=
*** Scenario 1
    - This scenario demonstrates login & logout functionality
      on vlabs landing pages
    - (Note : Do not use browser back button during the
      execution of this scenario) we are working on this
**** Login & Logout
    - Steps
      1. Visit the [[http://headr.vlabs.ac.in][url]]
      2. Click on =login= link and use below credentials
         + User name : example@vlabs.ac.in
         + Password : vlead123
      3. On successful login all disciplines will be
         displayed
      4. Click on =logout= link to exit from the current
         session
*** Scenario 2
    - This scenario demonstrates listing out all the labs of
      respective discipline
    - (Note : Do not use browser back button during the
      execution of this scenario) we are working on this

**** Show discipline wise labs
    - Steps
      1. Visit the [[http://header.vlabs.ac.in][url]]
      2. Click on =disciplines= menu item on top navigation
         bar and choose the discipline name among listed
      3. Click on =Computer Science and Engineering=
         discipline
      4. All labs related to the discipline will be
         displayed.
      5. Click on =Computer Programming= lab. This will take
         you to login page if you are not logged in already
      6. Course related to lab will be displayed.

      1. Visit the [[http://header.vlabs.ac.in][url]]
