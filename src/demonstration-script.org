*** Show discipline wise labs
    - Steps
      1. Visit the [[http://landingpage.vlabs.ac.in][url]]
      2. Click on =disciplines= menu item
      3. All disciplines will be displayed.
      4. Click on =Computer Science and Engineering= 
      5. All labs related to the discipline will be
         displayed.
      6. Click on =Computer Programming=
      7. Course related to lab will be displayed.

*** Show institute wise labs
    - Steps
      1. Visit the [[http://landingpage.vlabs.ac.in][url]]
      2. Click on =institutes= menu item
      3. All institutes will be displayed.
      4. Click on =IIIT-Hyderabad= 
      5. All labs related to the institute will be
         displayed.
      6. Click on =Computer Programming=
      7. Course related to lab will be displayed.

*** Show all labs
    - Steps
      1. Visit the [[http://landingpage.vlabs.ac.in][url]]
      2. Click on =alllabs= menu item
      3. All labs will be displayed.
      4. Click on =computer programming= 
      5. click on view course 

*** Search labs by lab names
    - Steps
      1. Visit the [[http://landingpage.vlabs.ac.in][url]]
      2. search the lab by name (search for computer programming) 
      3. It will display computer programming lab
      4. Click on view course
